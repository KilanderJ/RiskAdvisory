Savings Bank Model

Description

The Saving Bank Model provides an analysis of the interest rate risk in the Net Interest Income based on
  • Current commercial lending portfolio
  • Current commercial borrowing portfolio
  • Financial investment portfolio
  • Interest rate derivative portfolio (if any)
  • Financial borrowing (if any)
The model is based on historical sensitivity of lending rates and borrowing rates with respect to STIBOR3M. Details of commercial lending portfolio is also provided along with comparison with financial lending portfolio.

  • The 'Fill indata' tab below allows you to get instrument details and current lending and borrowing portfolio. You must provide the name of the Savings Bank on the Settings page in the indata template and ISIN codes on the portfolio tab.
  • The 'Run Model' tab allows you to run the model and retrieve a full report.
